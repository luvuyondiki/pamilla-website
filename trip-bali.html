Destination overview

Bali rewards travellers who mix upland culture with coastal downtime. We split nights between Ubud’s creative heart and a south-coast beach base so you experience both rhythms without constant one-way drives.

Private drivers on included days keep the group moving smoothly; you still get unscheduled hours for spa, surfing, or simply doing nothing by the pool.

Daily itinerary

Sample rhythm—exact temples and beach club access vary by season and ceremonies.

  1. Day 1

    Arrive Denpasar

    Airport meet, transfer to Ubud, welcome dinner briefing.

  2. Day 2

    Terraces at sunrise (included)

    Soft early start, guided walk, and café stop before a free afternoon.

  3. Day 3

    Temple & water palace

    Cultural dress guidance, respectful visiting pace, and local lunch.

  4. Day 4

    Artisan trail

    Woodcarving or silver village visit (rotating by group interest) and market time.

  5. Day 5

    Transfer to the coast

    Beach hotel check-in; sunset free time.

  6. Day 6

    Coastal hosted day

    Cliff-temple visit and swim-friendly beach stop with guide.

  7. Day 7

    Choose-your-pace

    Optional add-ons (yoga intensive, dive intro, beach club) or full rest day.

  8. Day 8

    Departures

    Breakfast and timed airport transfer.

Accommodation

Ubud stays lean toward boutique garden hotels with easy access to the central craft streets. On the coast we choose properties with reliable pool areas and shorter transfers to the cliff beaches you came for.

Room categories and resort names are itemised in your quote before you pay the deposit.

Activities included

  • Sunrise rice-terrace walk
  • Guided temple & water palace morning
  • Artisan village immersion
  • Coastal temple & beach day with guide

Optional add-ons

  • Balinese cooking class with market visit
  • Spa passport (three treatments package)
  • Surf lesson or SUP session
  • Photography walk at golden hour
