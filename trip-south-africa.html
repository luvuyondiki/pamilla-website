Destination overview

South Africa is one of the few places where world-class cities, dramatic coastlines, and authentic wildlife viewing sit within a few hours of each other. We anchor in Cape Town, add a structured safari window in a private or community-linked reserve, and weave in a winelands day for balance.

UbuntuVoyage handles the sequencing, transfers between regions, and pre-trip checklist (insurance reminders, packing notes, and tipping guidance).

Daily itinerary

Sample rhythm—lodges and reserve choice are confirmed in your quote.

  1. Day 1

    Welcome to Cape Town

    Airport transfer, hotel check-in, and sunset orientation walk along the promenade.

  2. Day 2

    City & Table Mountain

    Cable-car weather window, Bo-Kaap photo walk, and free evening for restaurants.

  3. Day 3

    Cape Peninsula

    Chapman’s Peak route, penguin beach time, and Cape Point visit with guide.

  4. Day 4

    Winelands hosted day

    Two boutique tastings, farm lunch, and return to Cape Town.

  5. Day 5

    Flight to safari hub

    Short domestic connection, lodge welcome, and sunset vehicle briefing.

  6. Day 6

    Full safari day (included)

    Morning and afternoon game drives with ranger-tracker team; meals at the lodge.

  7. Day 7

    Departures

    Road transfer to airport for onward international flights.

Accommodation

In Cape Town we favour sea-or-mountain-view rooms in safe, central areas with easy access to the V&A Waterfront and dining strips. On safari we work with small lodges that prioritise guiding quality, vehicle comfort, and ethical wildlife practices.

Lodge tier (classic vs premium) adjusts the from-price; we always show both on your written quote.

Activities included

  • Table Mountain visit (weather permitting)
  • Cape Peninsula guided day
  • Winelands tasting route
  • Multiple shared game drives on safari

Optional add-ons

  • Robben Island tickets (seasonal availability)
  • Shark-cage viewing morning (adrenaline option)
  • Hot-air balloon (safari region dependent)
  • Private photographer on safari day
