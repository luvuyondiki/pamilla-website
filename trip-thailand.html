Destination overview

Thailand pairs high-energy city days with slow coastal chapters. We sequence the trip so you land with an easy welcome day, build into iconic sights and a long-tail bay experience, then unwind on an island before a simple departure path home.

You travel with a small group, set departure windows across the year, and a dedicated UbuntuVoyage coordinator for pre-trip questions and on-trip support.

Daily itinerary

Sample rhythm—final timings and hotels are confirmed in your personalised quote.

  1. Day 1

    Arrivals & welcome

    Airport meet-and-greet, transfer to your Bangkok hotel, and an optional light evening walk for jet-lag reset.

  2. Day 2

    Temples & river city

    Guided morning at key river temples, local lunch, and free time for shopping or spa.

  3. Day 3

    Markets after dark

    Street-food-forward evening with hosts who explain dishes and how to order like a regular.

  4. Day 4

    Coast transfer

    Private road + boat connection to the bay area; sunset free time.

  5. Day 5

    Long-tail bay day (included)

    Shared long-tail exploration of limestone scenery, swimming stops, and beach picnic.

  6. Days 6–8

    Island pace

    Beach time, optional add-ons (diving, cooking class, kayak), and one hosted group dinner.

  7. Day 9

    Return toward Bangkok

    Scenic transfer and final-night city hotel for easy airport access.

  8. Day 10

    Departures

    Breakfast and timed airport transfers.

Accommodation

We prioritise walkable neighbourhoods, reliable air-conditioning, and strong guest reviews. In Bangkok you stay near transit links; on the coast and island we choose properties with pool access and comfortable beds after active days.

Exact property names are shared at booking so you know precisely what to expect.

Activities included

  • River temple half-day with guide
  • Hosted evening market food walk
  • Long-tail limestone bay day
  • Group sunset dinner on the island

Optional add-ons

  • Thai cooking class with market shop
  • Intro scuba or snorkel package
  • Spa & massage credits
  • Photography hour (couples & solo travellers)
